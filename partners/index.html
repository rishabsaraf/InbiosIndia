Inbios International Inc

InBios is a leading biotechnology company based in Seattle, Washington (USA). They specialize in the design, development, and manufacture of immunodiagnostic devices for infectious diseases. As such, they have specifically targeted those diseases that afflict tens of millions of people worldwide. They serve the global public marketplace by providing superior quality products that are accurate, easy to use and cost effective. Fuller Laboratories

Fuller Laboratories develops both clinical and veterinary assays for the diagnosis of vector-borne and zoonotic diseases. For more than 2 decades, Fuller Laboratories has been an innovator in the development of infectious disease serology products, delivering unique formats in ever-increasing sophistication.
